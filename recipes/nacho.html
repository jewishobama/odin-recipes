Odin's Pizza

Description

This nacho was made by the god of the nacho. A sorcerer created by Gaia god of the earth and love. The sorcerer put all of his magic to create this nacho that is now the Odin's favorite dish.

All of the Odin's family including Thor and Loki were all very happy to eat the fabulous godly nacho from the sorcerer.

Ingredients

  • Cheese from the Godly Cow
  • Tomato sauce from the King Tomahoh
  • Pepperoni freshly cut from the Heaven Garden
  • Crusty/Nachy chips straight from Hernest Oven

Steps

  1. Preheat Hernest Oven at 4956 degree
  2. Cut the King Tomahoh tomatoes in dice
  3. Mix the tomatoes in a 15432 gallon jar till sauce
  4. Put the tomato sauce over the Crusty/Nachy chips
  5. Pour the Godly Cow cheese over the tomato sauce
  6. Put fresh pepperoni over the cheese
  7. Nicely put the nacho in the oven for 29 minutes
  8. Enjoy the meal!
