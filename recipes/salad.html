Odin's Salad

Description

This salad was made by the god of the salad. A sorcerer created by Gaia god of the earth and love. The sorcerer put all of his magic to create this salad that is now the Odin's favorite dish.

All of the Odin's family including Thor and Loki were all very happy to eat the fabulous godly salad from the sorcerer.

Ingredients

  • Cheese from the Godly Cow
  • Tomato dice from the King Tomahoh
  • Persia freshly cut from the Heaven Garden
  • Onion crust straight from Hernest Collection

Steps

  1. Cut the King Tomahoh tomatoes in dice
  2. Mix the tomatoes in a 15432 gallon jar
  3. Put the tomato dice with the onion crust
  4. Pour the Godly Cow cheese over the tomatoes
  5. Put fresh persia over the cheese
  6. Nicely put the salad in the freezer for 27 minutes
  7. Enjoy the salad!
